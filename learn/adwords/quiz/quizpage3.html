Quiz: Creating Ads & Negative Keywords

Which line of ad text would Google disapprove based on their advertising policies?






The correct answer is #3 - Google does not allow ads to say "click here".

An advertiser would like to increase their click-through rate (CTR). Which would help them eliminate irrelevant impressions?






The correct answer is the #3: negative keywords will help you show for less irrelevant keywords, thus eliminating irrelevant impressions and increasing the percentage of users that click through to your ad.

When reviewing the Search Terms report, you notice several terms that aren't relevant to what you are advertising. How can you use this information when refining campaign's keywords?






The correct answer is #2. Adding the irrelevant terms as negative keywords will prevent your ads showing up for similar search queries.
